Lean Management

Project information

  • Category: Mobile
  • Language: AppScript
  • Based on: Appsheet

Multiples prototypes of mobiles applications, built on Google WorkSpace with AppSheet, it's a easy solution designed to adopt Lean Culture and enhance the management of Defects and Audits.

This combination of apps empowers Lean Management, Risk detection and improve communicarion between workers, technicians, and quality control teams to efficiently manage their equipment, measurements, and production processes, ensuring TeamWork and compliance with industry standards and Lean philosophy.
