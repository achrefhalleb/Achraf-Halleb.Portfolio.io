PPE Detection

Project information

  • Category: Artificial Intelligence
  • Language: Python
  • Based on: OpenCV & YOLO
  • Dataset Source: Roboflow

This Python PPE Detection Module is a sophisticated and vital tool designed to enhance workplace safety by automatically detecting multiple Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) items worn by individuals.

This module employs cutting-edge computer vision and image processing techniques to ensure that PPE compliance is maintained in various settings, from construction sites and industrial facilities to healthcare environments.
