AI Coach

Project information

  • Category: Artificial Intelligence
  • Language: Python
  • Based on: OpenCV & Mediapipe

This AI Coach module, meticulously crafted using Python, OpenCV, and MediaPipe, is your ultimate companion in achieving fitness and wellness goals. This small but powerful application harnesses the capabilities of artificial intelligence to detect angles and count repetitions during your exercise routines, ensuring that you maintain proper form and stay on track.

First i've created an Aim-Module to detect Positionsand draw Landmarks on Human body Parts.

Then, we can extract angles between different landmarks.

In this sample i worked on AI coach to control Dumbell and Squat exercices, i added a Tracking Bar and counting box.

We decide Angles limits before running the app, or we can extract it from professional coach's videos.

Tracking exercies can be on live videos or saved videos and photos too.
